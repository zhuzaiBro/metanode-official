国内IT困境，如何延续技术价值？

  • 国内互联网行业岗位竞争激烈，申请比达1：200，开发者面临更高要求。
  • 国内开发者易被替代，技术门槛低。
  • 技术出海成趋势，但链接海外远程岗位信息受限
  • 当下经济形势，选择三四线城市远程办公更合理。区块链提供新的远程工作机会，发展潜力大。

新人如何转型？

  • 确定转型方向

    包括区块链后端(Golang以太坊开发)、智能合约开发和DApp前端开发。

  • 补充技术栈

    现在对新进开发者的要求包括对技术架构的深入理解和具备一定的web3经验。

  • 获取岗位资源

    跳出国内招聘平台困境，通过海外区块链远程工作渠道直接应聘。

  • 重视圈子渠道

    区块链招聘方倾向于从社区或者人脉圈捞人才。

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most frequently asked questions.

  • What is Indie hackers?

    Indie hackers, also known as indie developers, and perhaps because developers don't carry enough weight, the term hacker is used here to mean very experienced tech developers who win financial independence, creative freedom, and a schedule to act on that they can call their own shots by creating online applications that can be monetized. In addition, an Indie Hacker is not necessarily just one person, but can be a small team of several people.

  • Who we are?

    RemoteCodeCamp is an open-source learning community co-created by Web3 enthusiasts. It serves as a bridge for beginners and Web2 developers transitioning to Web3. We focus on popularizing Web3 technologies, including but not limited to basic Solidity, advanced Solidity, OpenZeppelin standard contracts, and contract security. RCC joins hands with you to explore the uncharted territory of Web3.

  • About Nomoslabs?

    Founded in 2018 and headquartered in Dubai, Nomoslabs is a comprehensive incubator engaged in Web3 content incubation, public chain technology cooperation, and hackathon training. Nomoslabs has deep technical cooperation with Dfinity, Boka, Arb, and other public chains, and has cumulatively submitted public chain grants of 20+, and received grants totaling more than $300,000 USD. Nomoslabs has also provided technical consulting services for more than 20+ projects, and obtained a total of $4 million in financing.

  • What can I do with a web3 base already?

    If you have some basic knowledge, we are looking for people from the community to join us for the hackathon, or if you want to transition to being a training instructor you can contact an administrator for a review.

  • Does it require a payment to join?

    The RCC community is completely free to join, all the information can be obtained through feishu, if there is a question will also help people to answer, if you need 1V1 service will only incur the relevant costs.
